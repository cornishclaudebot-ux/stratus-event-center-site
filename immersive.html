Good to know

Questions, answered.

How many guests can Stratus hold?
The main hall flexes from intimate seated dinners to large-scale concerts with a full crowd. Share your headcount when you inquire and we'll confirm the right layout and capacity for your event.
How do I buy tickets to a show at Stratus?
Upcoming shows are listed with official sellers including Bandsintown, Ticketón, Tickeri, and Ticketmaster, with resale on StubHub. See the Tickets section above, or follow Club Stratus on Bandsintown for the live calendar.
Is parking available?
Yes. There is on-site lit parking, with easy access just off Indian School Rd and only minutes from the I-10 and Loop 101.
Can we bring our own caterer, or do you provide food and bar?
We offer in-house catering and licensed bar service, and we also work with approved outside caterers for cultural and specialty menus. We'll walk you through the options on your tour.
Do you provide security?
Every event at Stratus is staffed with professional on-site security. A safe, well-run room is the standard, not an add-on.
How do we reserve a date?
Start with an inquiry below or a quick call. After your tour we'll hold your date with a signed agreement and deposit. Popular weekends book out early, so the sooner you reach out, the better.
